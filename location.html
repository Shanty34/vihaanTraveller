MAJNU KA TILA

Majnu-ka-tilla is a colony in Delhi, India that was established around 1950. Majnu-ka-tilla
is officially called New Aruna Nagar Colony, Chungtown, and Samyeling. It is part of North Delhi
district and is located at the bank of the Yamuna River near ISBT Kashmiri Gate.
